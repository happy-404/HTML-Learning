Block & Inline HTML

Block Elements

  • Always starts on a new Line
  • Take Up the full width available on a webpage.
  • Browsers automatically add some space before and after the element
  • For Example: p >, div >, h1 - h6 > , nav > , >ul, >ol, >dd , >form , >hr, >pre tags are Block level elements.
